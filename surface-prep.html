Surface Prep

A flawless finish starts with proper preparation. At Belko Auto Body, we use industrial-grade sandblasting to remove rust, old paint, and debris—creating clean, smooth surfaces ready for paint or repair. This essential step improves adhesion and extends the life of your vehicle's finish. With our experienced team and reliable equipment, we ensure your commercial vehicle gets the durable foundation it needs for lasting performance and exceptional results on the road.

Our Process

Proper surface prep is where a quality finish begins. We remove rust, old paint, and contaminants, then smooth and level the panel so primer and paint can bond the way they’re supposed to. The result is better adhesion, cleaner lines, and a finish that lasts.

Whether it’s sanding, feathering edges, masking, or blasting, our process is designed to prevent peeling, bubbling, and premature wear—especially on commercial vehicles that take a beating.
